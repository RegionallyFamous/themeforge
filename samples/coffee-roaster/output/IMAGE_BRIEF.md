# Image Brief — Bellwether Coffee

This brief lists every image slot the theme uses. Replace the
generated placeholders before publishing the demo. Suggested
specifications are minimums; larger is fine.

## Hero

| Slot id           | Where it appears   | Aspect | Min size  | Suggested subject |
| ----------------- | ------------------ | ------ | --------- | ----------------- |
| `hero_lifestyle`  | Homepage hero      | 4:5    | 1200×1500 | Hands holding a portafilter, soft natural light, neutral backdrop. Or: dark roasted beans cascading from a scoop. Editorial, restrained. |

## Category showcase

| Slot id              | Aspect | Min size  | Suggested subject |
| -------------------- | ------ | --------- | ----------------- |
| `category_single`    | 1:1    | 1000×1000 | Bag of coffee on a clean surface, soft shadow. |
| `category_subs`      | 1:1    | 1000×1000 | Stack of three coffee bags, top-down. |
| `category_gear`      | 1:1    | 1000×1000 | Hario V60 or Chemex, lightly used, on a wooden surface. |

## Editorial

| Slot id           | Where it appears        | Aspect | Min size  | Suggested subject |
| ----------------- | ----------------------- | ------ | --------- | ----------------- |
| `editorial_roast` | "About the roast" block | 16:10  | 1600×1000 | Roaster at the drum roaster, motion in hand, warm tones. |
| `editorial_farm`  | "Where it comes from"   | 16:10  | 1600×1000 | Coffee cherries on the branch, dappled light. |

## Product placeholders

The theme ships placeholder products with no images. The first import
pass should add at least three real product photographs.

| Slot id            | Aspect | Min size  | Suggested subject |
| ------------------ | ------ | --------- | ----------------- |
| `product_default`  | 1:1    | 1200×1200 | Coffee bag at slight angle, neutral seamless background. |

## Notes

- Color: lean warm. Browns, creams, soft sage. Avoid cold blue tones.
- Mood: heritage, considered, hand-made. Not slick, not aspirational.
- People: hands and craft over portraits. If portraits, soft and contextual.
- Don't use stock photography that looks stocky. Better to ship
  fewer, real photographs than more generic ones.
